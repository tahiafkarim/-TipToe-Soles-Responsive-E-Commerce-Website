Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging. Items must be in new condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for exchanges, and a small fee applies for refunds.

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $50. International shipping may take 7-14 business days.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account or using our order tracking tool on the website.
